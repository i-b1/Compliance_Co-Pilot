Key Demo Scenario

The deliberate gap: GDPR Art. 33
vs. internal policy POL-DATA-001

GDPR Article 33(1)

Regulator says:

"The controller shall notify the supervisory authority… without undue delay and, where feasible, not later than 72 hours after having become aware of a personal data breach."

Source: GDPR Art. 33(1) · Official Text
POL-DATA-001 §4.3

We say:

"The Information Security team shall notify the Privacy Officer within 5 business days of discovering a suspected data breach."

Source: Data Handling Policy POL-DATA-001 §4.3
⚠ HIGH

Gap: Internal policy allows up to 5 business days (~120h), which violates GDPR's 72-hour mandatory deadline by up to 48 hours minimum. The orchestrator detects this, flags severity HIGH, and offers to file a remediation issue.

Judging Criterion · 20%

Multi-step Reasoning

① Triage

Orchestrator analyses the user question and determines which regulations and policy domains are relevant before delegating.

② Independent Inquiry

Regulation IQ and Internal Policy IQ are called independently — neither sees the other's answer. This prevents cross-contamination.

③ Structured Diff

Orchestrator reasons over two independent, cited answers and produces a structured diff with: topic, clauses, severity (LOW/MED/HIGH), recommendation.

④ Adaptive Routing

If severity is LOW, the flow ends. If MED or HIGH, orchestrator offers a GitHub action — conditional multi-step logic.

⑤ Agentic Write

On confirmation, orchestrator calls GitHub MCP with a composed issue: severity prefix, both citations, recommended remediation, and appropriate labels.

Judging Criterion · 20%

Accuracy & Relevance

📋 Regulation IQ — Grounded Only

  • Knowledge source: Foundry IQ → SharePoint Compliance/Regulations/
  • Documents: GDPR summary PDF, HIPAA Privacy Rule PDF, SOC2 Trust Criteria PDF
  • General knowledge disabled (useModelKnowledge: false) — cannot hallucinate regulatory text
  • Instructions: "Never speculate beyond cited text"
  • Every answer must cite exact article number + document name

🏢 Internal Policy IQ — Grounded Only

  • Knowledge source: Foundry IQ → SharePoint Compliance/InternalPolicies/
  • Documents: Data Handling Policy, Retention Standard, Access Control Baseline
  • General knowledge disabled (useModelKnowledge: false) — cannot invent policy clauses
  • Instructions: "Never invent policy text"
  • Every answer must cite exact document title + section number

Foundry IQ semantic search grounds both sub-agents in SharePoint content with full RAG citation — the orchestrator is incapable of producing uncited claims.
